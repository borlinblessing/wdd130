Nigeria

Nigeria, Africa's most populous nation and largest economy, is a captivating powerhouse boasting over 250 ethnic groups and more than 500 distinct languages. It is home to Nollywood, the world's second-largest film industry by volume. The nation has the highest rate of twin births in the world,specifically in the town of Igbo-Ora.Nigeria has one of the world's largest diversities of butterflies, with over 1,000 documented species. The country features the Osun-Osogbo Sacred Grove, a UNESCO World Heritage site representing deep indigenous traditions. The ancient Benin Kingdom, located in modern-day Nigeria, used streetlights powered by palm oil centuries ago. Nigeria is also the origin of Afrobeats, a musical genre dominating global music charts. It boasts the first black African Nobel Prize for Literature winner, Wole Soyinka, awarded in 1986.
