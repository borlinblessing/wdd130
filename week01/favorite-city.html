Facts About South Korea From The Web.

South Korea (Republic of Korea) is a highly developed East Asian nation with a population of over 51 million, known for its rapid modernization, top-tier technology, and vibrant culture. Roughly 70% of its land is mountainous, with a capital, Seoul, that holds half the population. It is famous for its "Pali-pali" (hurry, hurry) culture, tech innovation, and K-pop.
